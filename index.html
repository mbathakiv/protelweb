ABOUT US

Protel Studios Ltd stands out as a leading independent TV content and production company in Kenya, with offices in Uganda and Nigeria. Specializing in delivering creative solutions for productions of any genre or complexity, the experienced team consistently exceeds client expectations. Their facilities include the second-largest TV studio in Kenya, radio and audio studios, an 8-camera HD OB unit, a Steady Cam, and state-of-the-art post-production facilities. With a commitment to quality, Protel Studios offers a unique plan of action for each project, blending creativity, years of professional experience, and market research insights. From television and radio to online digital and mobile platforms, Protel Studios is dedicated to delivering exceptional results that captivate audiences across diverse media channels.
